CMMG MK47

7.62×39 · AR platform · In-house at TTM

The TTM CMMG MK47 brings 7.62×39 capability to a modern AR architecture. Engineered and manufactured at our headquarters, every receiver, barrel, and internal component is built on our workshop floor with full traceability from raw stock to finished weapon.

Designed for reliability across varied operating environments, the MK47 program combines proven cartridge performance with TTM's precision manufacturing standards.

Technical specifications

  • Cartridge: 7.62×39mm
  • Platform: CMMG MK47 (AR / AK hybrid)
  • Operating system: Carbine-length direct impingement
  • Barrel: 16.1″ medium taper · 1:10″ RH
  • Overall length: ~36.8″ / 33.5″ collapsed
  • Weight: ~7.2 lb unloaded
  • Magazines: Standard AK-pattern (rock-and-lock)
  • Muzzle velocity: ~710 m/s (M43-class)
  • Effective range: ~400 m
  • Receiver: Billet 7075, TTM-machined
  • Controls: AR fire controls · AK paddle mag release
  • Suppressor: 5/8×24 thread ready
  • Manufacturing: 100% TTM in-house

Manufacturing

Every MK47 built at TTM is serialized and tracked from raw material through final assembly. CNC programming, dimensional inspection, and functional validation happen at every stage before a rifle leaves our facility.

The platform pairs AR ergonomics with AK magazine logistics, using a .308-class bolt for 7.62×39 strength. Critical assemblies stay in-house under TTM tolerances.
