Ruger Precision

6.5 Creedmoor / .308 · Bolt-action · In-house at TTM

The TTM Ruger Precision is our long-range bolt-action program — chassis, barrel work, and action fitting handled entirely on the workshop floor. Built for precision roles where repeatable groups and clean trigger geometry matter more than rate of fire.

Each rifle is serialized, bedded, and validated before handoff. Internal studies document the bolt, magazine well, and fire-control layout for engineering and training reference.

Technical specifications

  • Class: Precision rifle
  • Cartridge: 6.5 Creedmoor / .308 Win (config)
  • Action: Bolt-action
  • Barrel: 24″ CHF free-floated (6.5 CM) · 1:8″ RH
  • Overall length: 43.3–46.8″ / ~35.6″ folded
  • Weight: ~10.7 lb unloaded (6.5 CM Gen 3)
  • Magazines: AICS / Magpul multi-mag interface · 10 rd typical
  • Muzzle velocity: ~820–860 m/s (6.5 CM, load dependent)
  • Effective range: 1,000+ m
  • Stock: Folding adjustable MSR chassis
  • Trigger: Adjustable marksman trigger
  • Suppressor: 5/8×24 thread ready
  • Manufacturing: 100% TTM in-house

Manufacturing

Precision platforms live and die on concentricity and bedding. TTM machines and fits the action interface, barrel tenon, and chassis hard-points in-house, then validates each rifle through inspection before it clears the floor.

Internal cutaways show the bolt, magazine path, and trigger group as assembled — the same reference format we use across the small-arms catalog. This is a marksman platform, not a volume-of-fire rifle.
