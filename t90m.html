T-90M "Proryv"

125 mm smoothbore · Integrated fire-control · V-92S2F propulsion

The TTM T-90M is a main battle tank engineered for high-intensity conflict. Yoni led turret geometry and fire-control integration; Micah directed the V-92S2F powerplant program. The platform combines composite armor, reactive protection, and a unified targeting suite.

Every major subsystem — turret, autoloader, engine, and suspension — is validated at our facility before deployment. The T-90M is currently fielded in Ukraine.

Technical specifications

  • Combat weight: ~46–48 tons
  • Main armament: 125 mm 2A46M-5 smoothbore
  • Secondary: 7.62 mm PKTM coax · 12.7 mm Kord AA MG
  • Ammunition: APFSDS, HE-FRAG, HEAT · ATGM capability
  • Engine: V-92S2F diesel, 1,130 hp
  • Transmission: Automatic (upgraded T-90M drivetrain)
  • Top speed: ~60 km/h road · ~45 km/h cross-country
  • Operational range: ~550 km
  • Protection: Composite armor + Relikt ERA · soft-kill APS suite
  • Crew: 3 (commander, gunner, driver) · autoloader
  • Fire control: Thermal sights, laser rangefinder, ballistic computer
  • Status: Deployed in Ukraine

Systems integration

The turret redesign improves target acquisition speed and ballistic protection through optimized armor angling. Fire control integrates thermal imaging, laser rangefinding, and ballistic computation into a single interface.

The V-92S2F powerplant delivers peak torque at lower RPMs for cross-country mobility. Suspension and stabilization systems support accurate fire-on-the-move engagement.

Lead engineer: Yoni · Propulsion: Micah
