TTM Beaver

7.62×39 · AK magazine compatible · Purpose-built for modern conflict

The TTM Beaver is a tactical evolution designed for the unique demands of the conflict in Ukraine — combining battle-proven architecture with modern ergonomics for high-intensity, mobile warfare.

Program lead: Yoni Shapiro, Lead Engineer

← All systems
Overview

Purpose-built for modern conflict

The Beaver is not just another rifle. It is a platform engineered specifically for operators who need reliability, mobility, and supply-chain resilience in theater.

Engineered for the frontline

The Beaver is deliberately optimized for weight reduction — every gram is functional. By removing unnecessary mass without sacrificing structural integrity, the platform minimizes fatigue during long patrols and rapid engagements.

Tactical versatility & mobility

  • Fully ambidextrous controls — seamless strong- and weak-side transitions for urban obstacle work.
  • Collapsible stock system — shrinks the rifle footprint for rapid ingress and egress from vehicles and APCs.
  • Logistical readiness — chambered in 7.62×39mm with standard AK magazine compatibility for ammunition commonality in theater.

Performance & maintenance

A refined short-stroke gas system re-engineers the gas port and piston interface for improved cycle consistency and reduced recoil impulse versus traditional platforms.

Field serviceability drove the design: a simplified barrel connection system enables rapid breakdown and cleaning — less downtime, more time in operation.

The TTM Beaver is the intersection of refined engineering and field-tested necessity. Built for those who need a rifle that works as hard as they do.
