Design philosophy

Four pillars

Every decision on the Beaver program traces back to one of these constraints — what operators actually need in theater, not what looks good on a spec sheet.

01

Mass budget

Malicious weight reduction — every gram is functional. Less fatigue on long patrols, faster transitions in contact.

02

Mobility

Collapsible stock and a compact envelope for rapid ingress and egress from vehicles, APCs, and tight urban spaces.

03

Logistics

7.62×39mm and standard AK magazines — ammunition commonality and supply-chain resilience where it matters.

04

Serviceability

Simplified barrel connection and a refined short-stroke gas system — breakdown and cleaning without a full armorer shop.

Overview

Purpose-built for the frontline

The Beaver is not just another rifle. It is a platform engineered for operators who need reliability, mobility, and logistical sanity under sustained operational tempo.

A refined short-stroke gas system re-engineers the gas port and piston interface for improved cycle consistency and reduced recoil impulse versus traditional platforms. Fully ambidextrous controls support seamless strong- and weak-side transitions — critical when maneuvering around urban obstacles and debris.

The TTM Beaver is the intersection of refined engineering and field-tested necessity. Built for those who need a rifle that works as hard as they do.

Platform rationale

Why not just run a standard AK?

Theater already has 7.62×39 and AK magazines in the supply chain. The Beaver keeps that logistics advantage — and fixes what legacy platforms compromise.

Legacy AK-pattern TTM Beaver
Weight discipline Unoptimized mass Functional gram budget
Vehicle / CQB footprint Fixed or basic stock Integrated collapsible stock
Ambidextrous use Right-biased controls Fully ambidextrous layout
Gas system consistency Traditional long-stroke variance Refined short-stroke tuning
Field breakdown Armorer-dependent service Simplified barrel connection
Ammo & magazines 7.62×39 · AK mags 7.62×39 · AK mags
Specifications

Program data

Technical direction

  • Cartridge: 7.62×39mm
  • Magazines: Standard AK-pattern (30 rd typical)
  • Operating system: Refined short-stroke gas piston
  • Barrel: 14.5″ target · simplified field-service connection
  • Overall length: 34.5″ / 26.2″ collapsed (target)
  • Weight: 7.4 lb unloaded (target)
  • Rate of fire: 600–700 RPM (target)
  • Muzzle velocity: ~715 m/s (M43-class)
  • Effective range: ~400 m
  • Controls: Fully ambidextrous
  • Stock: Integrated collapsible system
  • Status: In active development — specs provisional
  • Manufacturing: TTM in-house
  • Traceability: Forge OS program tracking
