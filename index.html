CAD Reference Library

A complete, high-detail CAD archive of 30+ firearm platforms — every entry modeled part-by-part, from receiver architecture and bolt carrier groups down to pins, springs, and fire-control components. Each platform is documented as a finished exterior study paired with a full internal cutaway, and the entire collection is maintained as a single controlled archive, revised as new studies are completed.

  • 30+Platforms in full CAD
  • 2×Exterior + cutaway per entry
  • 4Classes — sidearms to MGs

Special commissions · By vetting only

TTM Custom Works

One-off rifles and sidearms, engineered and built to a single client's specification. This is not a product line — it is a commissioning program. Every build starts with a clearance review, and every specification is negotiated directly with our engineers.

  • Custom rifles and handguns, designed from the receiver up
  • Client-specified caliber, architecture, furniture, and finish
  • Not commercially available — eligibility reviewed per request

Mechanical Engineer

Design and validate mechanical systems across armor and small-arms programs.

  • BS in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent experience
  • SolidWorks or similar CAD proficiency
  • Structural analysis and tolerance stack-up
  • Hands-on prototyping or test experience

CAD Designer

3D modeling, assemblies, and technical documentation for production.

  • Advanced 3D modeling (SolidWorks preferred)
  • GD&T and manufacturing-ready drawings
  • Version control and design revision discipline
  • Experience with machined or fabricated parts

Manufacturing Engineer

Process design, tooling, and production optimization on the workshop floor.

  • Process planning for CNC, fabrication, or assembly
  • Fixture design and workholding knowledge
  • Quality inspection and non-conformance handling
  • Comfort working directly with machinists

CNC Machinist

Precision machining for receivers, turret components, and critical assemblies.

  • 3-axis CNC operation and setup experience
  • Blueprint reading and micrometer-level inspection
  • Aluminum and steel machining familiarity
  • Reliability under tight tolerance requirements
