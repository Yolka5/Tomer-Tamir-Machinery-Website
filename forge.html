TTM FORGE OS

The manufacturing operating system for TTM's closed ecosystem. One platform connecting programs, machines, materials, and people across our headquarters — built by us, for how we actually work.

Employee login Join the build team
Capabilities

One system. The whole floor.

01 / PROGRAMS

Program dashboards

Live status for every system — Beaver, SIG SPEAR, T-90M, and beyond. Phase tracking from concept through deployment.

02 / PRODUCTION

Work orders

Create, assign, and track jobs across the workshop floor. Every operation logged against its program and part.

03 / MACHINES

Machine telemetry

Utilization, run state, and maintenance windows for CNC and fabrication equipment — at a glance.

04 / MATERIALS

Inventory & stock

Raw stock, consumables, and finished components with low-stock alerts tied to active work orders.

05 / QUALITY

QC & traceability

Serial-level traceability from raw material to finished weapon. Inspection records attached to every unit.

06 / PEOPLE

Team & access

Role-based access for engineers, machinists, and operations — everyone sees exactly what they need.

Why we're building it

Closed ecosystem. Total control.

Off-the-shelf manufacturing software is built for someone else's factory. FORGE OS is tailored to our HQ — our machines, our programs, our workflow. No outsourced code in the loop, matching the same in-house standard we hold for hardware.
