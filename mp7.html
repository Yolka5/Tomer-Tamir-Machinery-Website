Small Arms

MP7

4.6×30mm · PDW · In-house at TTM

The TTM MP7 is a compact personal defense weapon chambered in 4.6×30mm. Engineered at our headquarters, the platform delivers closed-bolt select-fire capability in a minimal footprint — suited for vehicle crews, security details, and close-quarters operations where soft-armor threats matter.

Every component is manufactured on our workshop floor. From receiver machining to final assembly, the MP7 program follows the same in-house standards as our full-size systems.

Technical specifications

  • Class: Personal defense weapon (PDW)
  • Cartridge: 4.6×30mm
  • Operating system: Short-stroke gas piston, closed bolt, rotating bolt
  • Barrel: 7.1″ (180 mm)
  • Overall length: 25.2″ extended / 16.3″ collapsed
  • Weight: ~4.2–4.4 lb unloaded
  • Magazines: 20 / 30 / 40 rd proprietary (grip feed)
  • Rate of fire: ~950 RPM
  • Muzzle velocity: ~680–735 m/s (load dependent)
  • Effective range: ~200 m
  • Stock: Retractable buttstock
  • Controls: Fully ambidextrous
  • Intended use: CQB, vehicle crew, security
  • Manufacturing: 100% TTM in-house

Manufacturing

The MP7 program leverages TTM's precision machining capability for tight-tolerance receiver and bolt assemblies. Each unit undergoes functional validation before leaving our facility.

The 4.6×30mm cartridge is optimized for soft-armor penetration in a PDW envelope — not a rifle substitute at distance. Compact platforms demand higher manufacturing discipline; every MP7 is built to the same standards as our full-size systems.
