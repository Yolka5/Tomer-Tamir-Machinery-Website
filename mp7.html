MP7

Personal defense weapon · Compact · In-house at TTM

The TTM MP7 is a compact personal defense weapon built for constrained environments. Engineered at our headquarters, the platform delivers select-fire capability in a minimal footprint — suited for vehicle crews, security details, and close-quarters operations.

Every component is manufactured on our workshop floor. From receiver machining to final assembly, the MP7 program follows the same in-house standards as our full-size systems.
