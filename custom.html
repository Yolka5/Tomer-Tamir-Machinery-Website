TTM Custom Works

A commissioning program for clients who need a weapon that doesn't exist yet. One-off rifles and sidearms, engineered from the receiver up to a single client's specification and built entirely on our workshop floor. These builds are not commercially available and never will be — each one is designed once, built once, and delivered to one client.

Scope

What we commission

01

Precision rifles

Purpose-built long guns — client-specified caliber, barrel profile, receiver architecture, and furniture.

02

Carbines & PDWs

Compact platforms configured for a specific role, from suppressed builds to non-standard calibers.

03

Sidearms

Custom pistols — slide work, frame modifications, fire-control tuning, and full ground-up builds.

04

Integrated packages

Complete weapon systems delivered with optics, suppressors, and fitted casework as one package.

Availability. Custom Works is not a store. There is no catalog, no pricing page, and no off-the-shelf option. Commissions are accepted from government agencies, defense contractors, and vetted private entities only — and we decline more inquiries than we accept.

Compliance. Every commission passes a legal and export-control review before design work begins. We do not proceed on any build that cannot be delivered lawfully.

How it works

From inquiry to handoff

01

Inquiry

Tell us who you are and what you need. Every inquiry is read by an engineer, not a sales team.

02

Vetting

Eligibility, legal, and export-control review. Approved clients receive a formal commission docket.

03

Design consult

Specification locked directly with our engineers — caliber, architecture, furniture, finish.

04

Build & handoff

Machined, assembled, and validated on our floor. Delivered with full documentation.
